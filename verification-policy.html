Verification Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

OwnSpace offers verification badges to help users identify reviewed accounts, creators, and businesses. Verification does not mean OwnSpace endorses a person, business, post, community, event, or place.

1. Verification Badge Types

  • Verified Account: A user has submitted required identity and age-related proof for review.
  • Verified Creator: A user has submitted Verified Account proof plus a creator/social media link.
  • Verified Business: A user has submitted Verified Account proof plus business ownership proof.

2. Verified Account Requirements

To request a Verified Account badge, you must submit:

  • Full legal name.
  • Username.
  • Email.
  • Date of birth and 18+ confirmation.
  • Government photo ID.
  • Selfie holding the handwritten daily verification code shown on OwnSpace.
  • Consent checkbox confirming the information is real and may be reviewed for verification.

3. Verified Creator Requirements

Verified Creator requires everything from Verified Account plus:

  • Social media link, creator profile, website, or other public creator page.

4. Verified Business Requirements

Verified Business requires everything from Verified Account plus:

  • Business name.
  • Business website or social page.
  • Business ownership proof such as registration proof, invoice, domain proof, business document, or other proof accepted by OwnSpace.

5. Upload Rules

  • Photos only.
  • Maximum file size: 1 MB per file.
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP.
  • No PDF uploads for now.
  • Images must be clear enough for review.
  • Daily handwritten code must match the code shown on the verification page for that day.

6. How Verification Review Works

  • OwnSpace may approve, reject, or request more information.
  • OwnSpace may reject requests that are unclear, incomplete, suspicious, inconsistent, unsafe, fake, or policy-violating.
  • OwnSpace may remove verification later if a user violates rules, impersonates someone, submits false information, loses eligibility, or creates safety risk.

7. Proof File Deletion

Your personal verification proof files are used only for review. After approval, rejection, or final decision, OwnSpace’s policy is to delete your ID/selfie/business proof files and keep only safe verification status and review history.

After decision, OwnSpace aims to keep only:

  • User ID.
  • Badge type.
  • Verification status.
  • Reviewed date.
  • Reviewer/admin ID.
  • Admin note or rejection reason if needed.
  • Proof deleted timestamp.
  • Safe audit history.

After final decision, OwnSpace aims to delete or clear:

  • Government ID photo.
  • Selfie holding code.
  • Business proof image.
  • Full legal name if no longer needed.
  • Full date of birth if no longer needed.

8. Privacy and Security

Verification proof is sensitive. OwnSpace stores verification uploads in a private storage area and limits access to the user and authorized admins where possible.

9. No Guarantee or Endorsement

Verification is not a guarantee that a user is safe, trustworthy, truthful, professional, or endorsed by OwnSpace. Users should still use caution when interacting online or offline.

10. False Information

Submitting fake ID, fake selfie, fake business proof, fake social profile, impersonation details, edited proof, or misleading information may lead to rejection, loss of Trust, suspension, or ban.

11. Appeals or More Information

If your request is rejected or marked “Need More Info,” you may be able to resubmit or provide updated information depending on the reason and account status.

12. Contact

For verification questions, contact: verification@theownspace.com
