Account Actions
Use temporary hide when you may return later. Permanent deletion is handled by admin review for safety.

Logout

Sign out from your current account on this device.

Temporarily Hide Account

Soft delete hides your profile from public areas, removes you from Nearby, makes your profile private, and keeps your account available for reactivation later.

This is reversible. Your data is not permanently deleted by this button.

Permanent Delete Request

Permanent deletion cannot safely be completed from the browser. This sends a request for admin review. After approval, admin can delete your Auth user and related records from the server side.

This creates a pending request in account_delete_requests. It does not directly delete your Supabase Auth account.
