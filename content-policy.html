Content Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

This policy explains what content is allowed, restricted, and prohibited on OwnSpace. It applies to posts, photos, captions, comments, messages, profiles, communities, events, places, verification, and reports.

1. Allowed Content

OwnSpace may allow natural lifestyle, body-positive, naturist, community, creator, event, place, educational, social, artistic, and mature content, as long as it follows consent, age, safety, privacy, and legal rules.

2. Consent and Age Rules

  • You must have permission to post every person shown in your content.
  • Every person in adult-sensitive, nude, intimate, or sexual content must be 18+.
  • Everyone shown in adult-sensitive content must have consented to the content being created and shared.
  • You must be able to remove content if someone withdraws consent or reports a valid issue.

3. Strictly Prohibited Content

The following content is not allowed and may result in immediate removal, report escalation, account suspension, or permanent ban.
  • Any sexual, nude, exploitative, or suggestive content involving minors.
  • Non-consensual intimate images or videos.
  • Revenge content, hidden-camera content, stolen/leaked content, blackmail content, or content shared to embarrass someone.
  • Threats, coercion, grooming, trafficking, exploitation, or forced activity.
  • Doxxing, private information, identity documents, addresses, phone numbers, workplace details, or private family information shared without permission.
  • Impersonation, fake verification, fake business claims, or deceptive profiles.
  • Sexual services, illegal services, illegal sales, trafficking, or illegal meetups.
  • Graphic violence, credible threats, self-harm encouragement, or dangerous instructions.
  • Hate, extremist praise, harassment, targeted abuse, or dehumanizing language.
  • Spam, scams, phishing, malware, fake engagement, fake referrals, bot activity, or manipulation of Trust/Points.

4. Restricted or Reviewable Content

Some content may be allowed only with limits or may require review:

  • Adult-sensitive content.
  • Public communities or events involving nudity, naturism, private meetups, or body-focused activities.
  • Places submitted as naturist/adult-friendly places.
  • Posts or profiles receiving multiple reports.
  • Content involving businesses, paid services, promotions, sponsorships, or affiliate links.

5. Profile Content

  • Profile photos, display names, usernames, bios, and links must not impersonate or mislead.
  • Profiles must not contain threats, hate, illegal offers, explicit solicitation, harassment, or private information of others.
  • Verified badges may be removed if verification is abused or misrepresented.

6. Communities

  • Community creators must set clear rules and remove harmful content where possible.
  • Public and private communities must still follow OwnSpace rules.
  • Private communities are not exempt from safety rules or moderation.
  • OwnSpace may remove, hide, suspend, or take over communities when needed for safety or policy enforcement.

7. Events and Places

  • Events and places must be described honestly.
  • No unsafe, illegal, exploitative, coercive, or misleading events/places.
  • OwnSpace does not verify every event or place and is not responsible for offline safety.
  • Users should use caution before attending any event or visiting any place.

8. Moderation and Enforcement

OwnSpace may remove, restrict, hide, or review content at any time. We may warn, suspend, ban, reduce Trust, remove points, block features, remove badges, reject verification, or preserve moderation history.

9. Appeals

If you believe moderation action was incorrect, contact us with your account information, content details, and explanation. We may or may not reverse the decision.

10. Contact

For content safety issues, contact: safety@theownspace.com
