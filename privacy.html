Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information OwnSpace collects, why we collect it, how we use it, how long we keep it, and how you can contact us.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use OwnSpace, we may collect:

  • Account information such as username, email, display name, profile photo, bio, settings, and login details.
  • Profile information such as public/private status, links, city/general location if you choose to add it, Trust Bar, points, badges, communities, events, and places.
  • User-generated content such as posts, captions, comments, messages, reports, communities, events, places, photos, and uploaded images.
  • Verification information such as legal name, date of birth, email, username, government ID photo, selfie holding a daily code, social link, business name, and business proof, depending on the badge requested.
  • Technical information such as device/browser data, IP address, logs, security events, error reports, and basic usage activity.
  • Communication and support information when you contact us.
  • Marketing consent information if you choose to receive promotional messages.

2. Why We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create, secure, and manage accounts.
  • Show profiles, posts, communities, events, places, and user interactions.
  • Operate Trust Bar, points, referrals, creation limits, badges, boosts, reports, and moderation systems.
  • Review verification requests and issue badges.
  • Detect spam, fraud, abuse, impersonation, safety risks, prohibited content, and policy violations.
  • Send notifications related to the app, your account, reports, verification, messages, communities, events, places, or safety updates.
  • Send marketing messages only where you have provided consent or where permitted by law.
  • Maintain logs and records needed for safety, legal, security, dispute, and moderation purposes.

3. Public Information

Some information may be visible to other users, such as display name, username, profile photo, bio, public posts, public communities, public events, public places, Trust Bar, points, badges, followers/following, and public activity depending on your settings.

4. Private and Sensitive Information

Private verification materials such as government ID, selfie proof, and business proof are used only for verification review. They are stored in a private bucket and are intended to be deleted after a final decision.

Government ID, selfie proof, business proof, full legal name, and full date of birth are sensitive. OwnSpace’s policy is to delete or clear these after approval, rejection, or final verification decision, keeping only safe verification status and audit history.

5. Verification Data Retention

After verification review, we aim to keep only:

  • User ID.
  • Badge type.
  • Status such as approved, rejected, pending, or need more info.
  • Review date, reviewer/admin ID, and admin note if needed.
  • Proof-deleted timestamp.
  • Safe audit history.

We aim to delete or clear government ID images, selfie proof images, business proof images, full legal name, and full DOB after a final decision unless we are required to retain something for legal, safety, fraud, security, or dispute reasons.

6. Messages and Notifications

OwnSpace may process message requests, chat messages, and notifications so users can communicate. We may review or restrict messages where needed for reports, safety, legal compliance, spam prevention, or abuse prevention.

7. Reports and Moderation Records

If content or an account is reported, we may keep report details, moderation decisions, admin notes, warning/suspension/ban history, hidden content records, and audit logs. These records help protect users and enforce rules.

8. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or disclose information when needed to operate the service, use hosting/storage/authentication providers, investigate abuse, respond to legal requests, protect users, enforce policies, or comply with law.

9. Storage and Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No system is perfect, and we cannot guarantee complete security. You should use a strong password and keep your login details safe.

10. Marketing Messages

Marketing consent is optional. If you choose to receive promotional emails or updates, you can unsubscribe later where required. We may still send important account, safety, security, or service messages that are not promotional.

11. Cookies, Analytics, and Local Storage

OwnSpace may use cookies, local storage, analytics, or similar technologies for login sessions, preferences, security, app performance, usage analytics, and fraud prevention.

12. Your Choices

  • You can edit some profile information in settings/profile pages.
  • You can choose public/private profile settings where available.
  • You can request deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal, safety, dispute, moderation, or security limits.
  • You can choose whether to provide verification information. Without required proof, verification cannot be completed.

13. Account Deletion

If account deletion is available or requested, some information may be deleted, anonymized, hidden, or retained where needed for safety, moderation, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute records.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If the update is important, we may ask you to accept the latest version before continuing to use OwnSpace.

15. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@theownspace.com
