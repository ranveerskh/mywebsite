Community Guidelines

Effective date: April 25, 2026

OwnSpace is built around being real, natural, respectful, and safe. These guidelines apply to profiles, posts, comments, messages, communities, events, places, reports, and all user activity.

1. Be Real and Respectful

  • Use your account honestly.
  • Do not impersonate someone else.
  • Respect different bodies, lifestyles, cultures, identities, communities, and boundaries.
  • Disagreement is allowed. Abuse is not.

2. Consent Is Required

Consent is one of the most important rules on OwnSpace.

  • Do not post anyone’s photo, video, intimate content, private message, or personal information without permission.
  • Do not pressure, threaten, blackmail, manipulate, or coerce anyone to create or share content.
  • For adult-sensitive content, every person shown must be 18+ and must have agreed to the content being shared.

3. No Minors in Adult Contexts

Any sexual, nude, exploitative, or suggestive content involving minors is strictly forbidden. This includes real, edited, AI-generated, fictionalized, or implied minor content.

4. No Harassment or Doxxing

  • No threats, stalking, intimidation, bullying, or targeted abuse.
  • No sharing someone’s address, workplace, phone number, private documents, family details, or other personal information without permission.
  • No encouraging others to harass, attack, shame, expose, or pressure someone.

5. No Hate or Dehumanization

Do not attack people based on race, ethnicity, religion, nationality, caste, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, body type, immigration status, or other protected/personal identity factors.

6. Safe Communities, Events, and Places

  • Community creators and event/place creators are responsible for setting clear expectations.
  • No unsafe meetups, illegal activities, scams, exploitation, or pressure-based events.
  • Admins may remove or restrict communities, events, places, or creators for safety reasons.
  • Very low Trust, active warnings, or active suspensions may block creation of communities, events, or places.

7. Trust Bar and Points

  • Trust Bar is based on safety, behavior, verification, valid reports, moderation history, and positive activity.
  • Points are separate from Trust.
  • Purchased points do not increase Trust.
  • Fake activity, spam, fake referrals, fake reports, or engagement manipulation may reduce Trust or lead to restrictions.

8. Reporting

Use reports to help keep OwnSpace safe. Do not submit fake, revenge, spam, or abusive reports. Valid reports may help improve community safety. Abusive reports may lead to action against the reporter.

9. Moderation Actions

OwnSpace may take action such as removing content, hiding profiles, limiting features, reducing Trust, removing points, warning, suspending, banning, deleting communities, rejecting verification, or saving moderation history.

10. Be Careful Offline

OwnSpace is not responsible for offline meetups. If you meet someone from OwnSpace, use caution, meet in safe public places where appropriate, tell someone you trust, and never ignore your safety instincts.

11. Contact

For safety concerns, contact: safety@theownspace.com
